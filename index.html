10 Ways to Enhance Your Personal Style

Developing your personal style does not have to be a complicated or intimidating process. With the right advice and a few simple tips, you can easily create an outfit that stands out from the crowd and reflects your personality. Here are 10 tips to help you enhance your personal style.

1. Know Your Body Type

Knowing your body type is one of the most important steps you can take in honing a personal style. Different pieces and there silhouettes flatter and work with different body types. Knowing your shape and which silhouettes look best on you will help you make informed fashion decisions.

2. Have A Signature Piece

Having a signature piece is great way to instantly add a unique element to your wardrobe. Whether it’s a pair of statement earrings or a daringly patterned scarf, pick a piece that adds a bit of flair to your look and make sure to add it to all of your favorite outfits.


3. Invest In Quality Pieces


Investing in quality pieces is key to creating your personal style. Quality pieces tend to last longer and look nicer after multiple wears. Investing in quality does not necessarily equate to spending more money. Investing in classic piece like a nice blazer that never goes out of style is a great way to start. 

4. Experiment With Color

Vibrant colors look great, so don’t be afraid to experiment with them and bring some life to your ensembles. However, if you’re not ready to dive in, start slow by adding subtle accents of color like a printed scarf or a colorful bold lip. You can also mix and match different textures to break up the color. 

5. Stick To Basics

Basics are the core of your wardrobe and will always be in style. Investing in key basics like a nice pair of jeans, a few tucked-in shirts, and a white blazer will help create versatile looks that transition from day to night. 

6. Incorporate Trends

Don’t be afraid to try out the latest fashion trends. Whether it’s something subtle like a new color palette or something bold like oversized sleeves, incorporating trends is a great way to update your style and keep it fresh. Just remember to pick and choose which trends work best for you and your body.

7. Dress Up And Dress Down

Your style should be flexible and you should be able to dress it up or dress it down depending on the occasion. A pair of jeans and a blazer can be dressed up with a pair of heels or the same look can be dressed down with a pair of sneakers and a statement necklace. With the right pieces, you can create both elevated and casual looks.

8. Accessorize

Accessorizing is a great way to complete any look. Incorporate jewelry accessories like earrings, necklaces, and hats to enhance your look. In addition to jewelry, pick up a few fun statement pieces like a colorful scarf or a printed belt to finish off the look.


9. Play Around

When using different pieces to create your look, don’t be afraid to play around and get creative. Try different color combinations, silhouettes, and patterns to see what works best for your body type. Experimenting with your style is a great way to create looks that are uniquely you.

10. Celebrate Your Style

Your style is like a reflection of your personality, so wear your looks proudly and show off your style. Don’t be afraid to make bold fashion choices that you are comfortable with, even if they are completely different from those around you. Celebrate your unique style and continue to explore different looks that make you feel confident.
